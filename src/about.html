About

Neurodiversity In Tech is an online Slack community dedicated to supporting neurodivergent professionals in the technology industry.

Our dynamic network enables members to network, navigate their careers, and empower each other while advocating for meaningful social change. It serves as a crucial hub for sharing experiences, offering support, and promoting inclusivity within the tech world.

Our aim is to create a welcoming environment where diversity is celebrated and every individual feels valued. It's a space committed to making the tech industry more inclusive and diverse, empowering members to advocate for themselves and each other.
